Credential Provider (32 and 64 bits) supporting Windows 7/8/8.1/10/2012(R2) (version 5.0.4.6)

To disable the Credential Provider, uninstall the multiOTP Credential Provider
from Windows, or download and execute multiOTPCredentialProvider-unregister.reg
