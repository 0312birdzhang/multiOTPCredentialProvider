Credential Provider (32 and 64 bits) supporting Windows 7/8/8.1/10/2012(R2) (version 5.0.6.1)

multiOTP Credential Provider for multiOTP is a free implementation of a V2 Credential Provider for the multiOTP strong two-factor authentication solution

To disable the Credential Provider, uninstall the multiOTP Credential Provider
from Windows, or download and execute multiOTPCredentialProvider-unregister.reg

Binary download: https://download.multiotp.net/credential-provider/
